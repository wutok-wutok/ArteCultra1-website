"CULTURES OF ARTECHE EASTERN SAMAR"

(February 14) VALENTINE'S DAY

This is the special day that celebrate with love ones specially to our parents here in arteche they celebrate romantic love,friendship and admiration celebrating that day by sending messages of love and affection to their partners. Here in arteche the police officers the way they celebrate by sending flowers to the girls that they want to give, and sometimes they give it to their parent and they spend time with their family and loveone's in the park and restaurant and they give some sweet foods like chocolate. And two couples celebrating by expressing their love to each other spending time with and spending time for their family.
