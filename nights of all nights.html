"CULTURES OF ARTECHE EASTERN SAMAR"

(AUGUST 29) "NIGHTS OF ALL NIGHTS"

It is base on cultural events. Cultural event's is designed for entertainment and enjoyment of a more or less wide audience related to some branch of art,culture or values. These event aim to propagate and disseminate cultural themes. These may refer to different arts, musicals, photography and literature. Nights of all Nights is one of the most awaited events in Artehe Eastern Samar. All the government employees in Arteche Eastern Samar is the participants of this events including the teacher's, police, fire workers and LGU's they are the main highlight of this event. Each of the team prepares a uniquely performance base on culture, through their musical and dance performance they give much more life and color of our culture.
