"CULTURES OF ARTECHE EASTERN SAMAR"

(MAY 1-3)"FLORES DE MAYO"

During whole month of May, from 1 to the last day of May in Arteche there was the Flores de Mayo(flowers of May) devotions to the Blessed Virgin Mary. There was also church masses.Also the Santacruzan(from the Spanish santa cruz, "holy cross") on last day of month,is the ritual pageant and honors the finding of the True Cross by Helena of Constantiple.
