"CULTURES OF ARTECHE EASTERN SAMAR"

"ALL SAINT DAY"(NOVEMBER 1)

Month of november,here in arteche is celebrated to ALL SOULS DAY and many people go to cemetary to gain a candle and polish the tombs of their late family the tradition of people who are able to gain a candle to remember their late relatives or family members. And many people carry different flowers or decorations for the tombs of their late relatives. November the priest and members go to the cemetary so that they will mass and to be able to land all the tombs that are present.
