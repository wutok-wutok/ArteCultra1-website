"CULTURES OF ARTECHE EASTERN SAMAR"

(AUGUST 27) PASIKAT

A Pasikat or talent competition a contest in which people compete by showcasing their talents, for example in singing,dancing, acrobatics and ETC. People living in Arteche Eastern Samar and other barangays are going in Arteche Central Gymnasium to watch the Amazing Performance of the participants. This is one of the Highlights of events in Arteche Eastern Samar.
