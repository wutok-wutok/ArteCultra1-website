"CULTURES OF ARTECHE EASTERN SAMAR"

("April 27")"ARTECHE FOUNDING ANIVERSARY"

The anniversary way to remember when was Arteche become a province of Eastern Samar, every month here in arteche consist of different celebration, for instance, every year on april 27 the local government here in arteche declared a free check-up and make different kinds of games for the locals to enjoy, and combines with the celebration is the big party with dances intermission numbers perform by different group of dancers, this dances include different genres like modern dances and traditional dances.
