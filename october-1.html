"CULTURES OF ARTECHE EASTERN SAMAR"

(October 29-30)" MOST HOLY ROSARY IN ARTECHE"

The feast of the Most Holy rosary is one of the tradition of artechehanon, In this tradition the locals wear the traditional clothing barong tagalog and Filipiniana and go to the church to attend the mass, and after the mass, they go around the whole town of arteche with the virgin mary to prove that this tradition still exist in this Era.
