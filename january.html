"CULTURES OF ARTECHE EASTERN SAMAR"



("JANUARY 10-11")SR.SANTO NINO

In january 10-11 they are celebreting SANTO NINO here in arteche the first thing that they do is to go to church and pray to God that they want to pray, festival held annually in january in honor of the Santo Nino (Holy Child or infant Jesus)and they are gathering and they go around the whole arteche, that they called "PROSESYON" while praying and thankful to God.
