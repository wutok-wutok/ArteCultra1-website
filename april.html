"CULTURES OF ARTECHE EASTERN SAMAR"

(APRIL 10-16)"HOLY WEEK"

In the month of April, the people here in Arteche celebrate the death of our Lord Jesus Christ. during thursday and friday every person is forbidden not to go to the farm or work during holy week. People living here in Attached go to church on Palm Sunday to attendmass and have their woven palm friends (palaspas) blessed by the priest. In holy Saturday also called Eater vigil. Christian religious observance that end the lenten season, failing on the day before Easter sunday. The observance commerates give Final day of Christ's death.
